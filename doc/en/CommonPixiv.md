<details><summary><strong>📆 Edit Birth Year </strong></summary>

**Click Login again, slide the screen, click your avatar, click your avatar again, and select Edit Profile.**

[Pixiv Profile Settings](https://www.pixiv.net/settings/profile) - Edit Profile - Birth Year

Change your birth year to **2000 or earlier** to ensure your account age is verified as 20+.

**Mine -> Source Management -> Click the 3-dot menu next to Pixiv Source -> Login -> Account Settings**
![img](./pic/PixivLoginUIBase.png)

[Pixiv User Settings](https://www.pixiv.net/settings/viewing) - Restrictions - Restrictive Works

Toggle visibility based on your preference: **Show R-18 works** and **Show R-18G works**.

If you're unsure of the difference, check this official guide: [What are work ratings?](https://www.pixiv.help/hc/en-us/articles/39125149371289)
